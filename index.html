"Best Damn Tacos in Town!"

Poco Loco started as a food truck, serving the "Best Damn Tacos in Town" to the Providence area in 2010. Our brick & mortar shop opened it's doors to Cranston RI in 2012, and has been satisfying local cravings for savory tortilla wrapped goodness ever since. Stop by for yourself and see why it's the "Best Damn Taco Shop in Town!"
